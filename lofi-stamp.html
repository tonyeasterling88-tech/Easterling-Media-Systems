The Build Note

Problem

Video finishing steps become easy to skip or vary when overlays, presets, naming, and export settings are handled manually.

Approach

LoFi Stamp explored watermark and CTA overlays, H.264 export, logs, presets, SEO support, and creator workflow hooks.

Current state

The tool remains private while the repeatable finishing workflow is refined.
