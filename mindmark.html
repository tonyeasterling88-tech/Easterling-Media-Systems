MindMark Project

Private journaling with gentle reflection.

MindMark is being built as a simple way to capture thoughts, reflections, and ideas without friction, then revisit what matters later.

Join Closed Testing Read the Build Note

The Problem: capturing thoughts should not feel heavy.

Friction

The original personal problem was needing a lighter way to capture thoughts and reflections when they show up.

Revisiting

The app is focused on helping people organize their thinking and return to ideas that matter.

Early feedback

MindMark is in closed testing, with a small group invited to try it before a wider public release.

The Current Build

Private journaling

The core product direction is structured journaling and reflection, not social posting or public content.

Closed testing intake

The site collects early-access requests with Google account email, device type, phone model, and optional source notes.

Feedback loop

The next focus is getting MindMark into early testers' hands and using that feedback to guide the next iteration.

Development Roadmap

Closed testing intake

Collect early tester requests through the site.

Early tester onboarding

Invite a small group to use the app before public availability.

Feedback review

Turn tester responses into the next product iteration.

Public path

Prepare for broader availability after closed testing.
